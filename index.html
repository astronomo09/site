What is Asology?

Palm reading is a popular way of assessing one's character and predicting the future. Asology provides users with the opportunity to get a more accurate reading using new techniques which take into account the naturally forming lines of one's soles. This website is still in it's beta phase.

Readings

Predictions

A general reading of your present and predictions for the future are summarised and presented in a way that one can understand. Any special events are also recognised and listed here.

Social Life

A reading of your social character and the relationships you have with your family and peers. Gives a sign about the people you should cherish and the ones you should avoid.

Love Life

Love being a very important part of our lives, we include a dedicated section for an in-depth reading and prediction of your love life. Both from the past and the future.

Financial

A reading on your monetary status. Work and business related readings with an analysis on any chances you should take or leave. Fortunes are searched and you are given an idea on what you should look forward to in the future.
